# competitive edge

Most household systems either report one aggregate number or rely on NILM disaggregation, which is probabilistic and depends on high-resolution sensing at the distribution board.  
Our approach is appliance-specific and direct: each low-cost node measures the connected load physically, then adds ML for prediction and anomaly context. This gives exact per-device telemetry first, and intelligent interpretation second.

## speaking points

- "Generic systems estimate. Our node measures."  
- "NILM tries to separate mixed signatures. We avoid mixing by design."  
- "The hardware cost is about INR 1,500 per node, so the method stays deployable for student and small-lab settings."
